Know Your Customer

Know your customer's needs and priorities based on the consumer results with Low, Medium, or High spending power.

1. Highest

Customers with the highest spending power. They are the most loyal customers and have the highest purchasing power.

2. Medium

Customers with average spending power. They are maybe loyal customers and have a medium purchasing power.

3. Lowest

Customers with the lowest spending power. They are not loyal customers and have the lowest purchasing power.
